Ques1. Difference between flex row and column

  • flex-direction: row (default):
  • The flex container's children are arranged horizontally (from left to right).
  • The main axis is the horizontal axis (x-axis).
  • The cross axis is the vertical axis (y-axis).
  • flex-direction: column:
  • The flex container's children are arranged vertically (from top to bottom).
  • The main axis is the vertical axis (y-axis).
  • The cross axis is the horizontal axis (x-axis).

Justify-content allows you to do what?

justify-content is used in CSS to align and distribute space between items along the main axis (horizontal in row or vertical in column) of a flex container. It helps in positioning and spacing the items within the container.
Common values:

  • flex-start: Aligns items at the beginning.
  • flex-end: Aligns items at the end.
  • center: Centers the items.
  • space-between: Distributes items with equal space between them.
  • space-around: Distributes items with equal space around them.

    Ques5.What is the z-index in CSS?

    z-index in CSS controls the stacking order of elements.
    Higher z-index values are placed in front of lower values,
    and it only works on elements with a position other than static.

    Ques6.What is the difference between padding and margin?

    Padding:

    space inside an element, between the content and its border. It increases the size of the element.

    Margin:

    The space outside an element, between its border and neighboring elements. It does not affect the element's size.
